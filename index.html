Developer Bio

Hi, I'm the sole developer behind Trades Arcade. I began coding in 2024 as a fun side hobby using Unity, and what started as a passion project has grown into a full-fledged creative journey. I design and develop every aspect of my games personally, ensuring quality and a unique style in each release. I love the challenges of game development and the joy of bringing innovative ideas to life.

Beyond game creation, I value continuous learning, community feedback, and the excitement of turning creative visions into interactive experiences. Thank you for supporting my work!
